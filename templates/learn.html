When to use it?

The subjunctive mood is used to express situations of uncertainty, doubt, opinion, or emotion.

Example:
Je ne suis pas sûr que ce magasin soit ouvert jusqu'à 20h.
Translation: I'm not sure this shop is open till 8pm.

How to form it?

  1. Take the infinitive form of your verb (e.g., regarder).
  2. Put your verb in the 3rd person plural in the present tense (e.g., ils regardent).
  3. Remove the ending (e.g., regard).
  4. Add the correct ending based on the subject pronoun.

Endings:

  • je -e, tu -es, il/elle/on -e, nous -ions, vous -iez, ils/elles -ent
